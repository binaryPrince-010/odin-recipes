Chicken-Burger Recipes

The most popular Chiken Burger

This is the most popular chicken burger in our entire country.

Today I am going to show you how to make chicken burger.


Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • Lettuce leaves, sliced tomato, and condiments of your choice (e.g. mayonnaise, mustard, ketchup)

Instruction

  1. Preheat your grill or a grill pan to medium-high heat.
  2. In a small bowl, mix together the garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, oregano, and a pinch of salt and pepper.
  3. Season both sides of the chicken breasts with the spice mix.
  4. Place the chicken on the grill and cook for 6-8 minutes per side, or until fully cooked and no longer pink in the middle.
  5. Toast the hamburger buns on the grill for 1-2 minutes.
  6. To assemble the burgers, spread your desired condiments on the bottom buns.
  7. Top each bottom bun with a lettuce leaf and a slice of tomato, then place a chicken breast on top.
  8. Finish with the top buns and serve immediately. Enjoy your delicious chicken burger!
